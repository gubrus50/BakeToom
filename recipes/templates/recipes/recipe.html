Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celcius / GAS 5.
  2. Wash, peel, core and thinly slice the apples into wedges.
  3. Place the apples in the bottom of the ovenproof dish, sprinkle with 25g of sugar.
  4. Sift flour into mixing bowl, add butter then rub in to achieve fine breadcrumbs.
  5. Stir in 25g of sugar.
  6. Sprinkle the crumble mixture over the apples.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
